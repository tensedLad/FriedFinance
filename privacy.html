Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect site functionality. Most browsers allow you to:

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our Site. Google Analytics collects information anonymously and reports website trends without identifying individual visitors. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We display advertisements on our website through Google AdSense. This helps us keep our content free for all users.

Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites on the Internet. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites.

  • Non-personalised ads: If you choose "Necessary Only" cookies, you will still see ads, but they will not be based on your browsing history.

Opting Out of Personalised Advertising

You can opt out of personalised advertising in several ways:
