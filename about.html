Our Story

FriedFinance was founded in Frankfurt in 2014 with a simple mission: make financial information accessible to everyone. What started as a small team of financial journalists and analysts has grown into a global company with over 400 employees across Europe, the UK, and North America.

Our founders recognised a fundamental problem: financial decisions affect everyone, yet most guidance was either too complex, hidden behind paywalls, or biased by product sales. They believed that honest, clear financial education should be available to all - not just those who could afford expensive advisors.

Today, FriedFinance serves millions of readers annually. Our team includes qualified accountants, former banking professionals, tax specialists, and experienced financial journalists. Every article is researched by experts, fact-checked against official sources, and written in plain English.

Our UK division, headquartered in London, focuses exclusively on British finance: HMRC regulations, FCA-regulated products, and UK-specific guidance. We understand that UK tax and financial rules are unique, and we've built a dedicated team to cover them properly.

Our mission remains unchanged: help everyone make better financial decisions. All our core content is free, with honest comparisons and real examples.

Editorial Policy

All content on FriedFinance is written by our editorial team and reviewed for accuracy. We are committed to providing unbiased, factual information about UK personal and business finance.

  • Content is for educational purposes only and does not constitute financial advice
  • We are not authorised or regulated by the FCA
  • All facts are verified against official sources (HMRC, FCA, Companies House)
  • Articles are updated regularly to reflect current rates and regulations
  • Affiliate relationships never influence our editorial recommendations

How We Make Money

FriedFinance is free to use. We earn revenue through:

  • Display advertising via Google AdSense - non-intrusive ads that help fund our free content

Our promise: Editorial content is never influenced by advertisers or affiliate partners. We recommend only products we would use ourselves.
